Kemish W. Hendershot

Director of IT / Software Development

I attended Boise State University and Western Governors University, have a degree in Business Management, and speak Spanish. I am a self-taught software developer specializing in PHP, Angular, Node.js, and Python. I prefer a Mac or Linux to Windows. I think technology is amazing when used in the right ways but can be taken to extremes and can cause many problems; I therefore strongly advocate for the wise use of technology.

I love the Idaho outdoors and appreciate being close to so many outdoor activities. When I am not working, I enjoy mountain biking, kayaking or whitewater rafting, skiing, hunting, fly fishing, and, most of all, spending time with my family.
